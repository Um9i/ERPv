ERPv

A simple, open‑source ERP for small & medium businesses.

Try Demo Documentation GitHub

Features Overview

Inventory

Maintain accurate stock levels across locations, set low‑stock alerts, and search items by SKU or name. Works with batch/serial tracking.

Procurement

Streamline purchasing with reusable templates, supplier records, automated receipt matching and cost tracking.

Sales

Generate quotes and orders, manage customer profiles, and print invoices or export data for accounting.

Production

Build product structures, plan work orders, and track labor/material usage through the manufacturing lifecycle.
