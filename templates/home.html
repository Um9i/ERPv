Capabilities

Everything you need to run your operations

Five integrated modules designed to work together seamlessly.

Inventory

Maintain accurate stock levels across locations, set low‑stock alerts, and search items by SKU or name. Supports batch and serial tracking.

Procurement

Streamline purchasing with reusable templates, supplier records, automated receipt matching and cost tracking.

Sales

Generate quotes and orders, manage customer profiles, and print invoices or export data for accounting.

Production

Build product structures, plan work orders, and track labor and material usage through the manufacturing lifecycle.

Finance

Manage financial operations, track expenses, generate reports, and ensure compliance with accounting standards.
